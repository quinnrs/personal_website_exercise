I then returned home and attended the University of Tennessee. My corporate career as with DuPont and Conoco. I retired in 2003 to the Texas Hill Country. and have been examining my life and purpose, pursuing hobbies, and working on becoming a better person, husband, father, and grandfather. My wife and I moved to Charlotte in 2020 to be closer to family. I am very blessed.
